Welcome

Thanks for checking out the new halfacrossword blog. The next couple of weeks are going to be spent bug checking the new site. But as soon as I have the chance, I’ll be uploading new articles weekly.

I’m mostly interested in using tech in the classroom so that will be the main focus of the site. I’ll post some articles about how to get the most use out of the templates on the site, including practical ideas for the classroom.

I’m teaching in Yangon, Myanmar at the moment and I’ll probably include some posts with some thoughts about teaching here and how I’m getting on.

Keep checking back for new content and lesson ideas.

Glen
